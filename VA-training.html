📚 Core Product Knowledge
IUL — Index Universal Life (Premium Product)
Growth: 7-8% tied to the market
Living Benefits: Pays out if you have heart attack, stroke, or critical illness WHILE ALIVE
Best For: Healthy clients
Top Carriers: Mutual of Omaha, Foresters, Transamerica
Whole Life (For Sick Clients)
Growth: Fixed 3%
Living Benefits: None
Best For: Sick/unhealthy people who can't qualify for IUL
Cost: Expensive (~$100/mo for $10k coverage)
Top Carriers: Mutual of Omaha, Foresters, American Home Life
PMI vs. Mortgage Protection (CRITICAL DIFFERENCE)
PMI Mortgage Protection (What We Sell)
Protects the BANK if you default Protects your FAMILY if you die or get critically ill
Paid TO the bank when you die Paid TO your family to pay off the house
🎯 Lead Qualification Checklist
Must-Ask Questions (In Order)
Question 1: "Do you currently have any life or mortgage protection?"
If yes → get carrier and monthly payment.
Question 2: "Are you the decision-maker, or does someone else (spouse, etc.) need to be on the line?"
If spouse needed → GET THEM ON NOW. Don't accept callbacks.
Question 3: "What's the approximate mortgage balance on your home?"
They need at least $200k coverage.
Question 4: "Do you have any major health issues we should know about?"
This tells you IUL vs. Whole Life.
👻 The Ghost Analogy (Deepening the Why)
DEEPENING EMOTIONAL DISCOVERY
"Let's say you passed away yesterday, and I'm talking to your ghost right now. We are looking at this from the outside in. What is your wife doing for money to pay the mortgage today?"
This is NOT a close. This is forcing them to visualize the nightmare so Keith can build the closing later.
📅 Appointment Booking Rules
The Security Code (NO-SHOW KILLER)
DO NOT SKIP
If they won't grab a pen and write it down, they WILL NOT SHOW UP.
Script: "Grab a pen. Your security code is [LAST 4]. Read it back to me."

🟡 Evening (5-7pm): After work. Good secondary.

Multi-Decision Makers (SPOUSE TIE-DOWN)
MANDATORY

Script: "Keith requires both of you on the line. What time today or tomorrow are you both home together?"
