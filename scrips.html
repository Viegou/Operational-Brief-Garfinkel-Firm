SECTION 1 — AGED LEAD SCRIPT (The Opener)

Step 1: The Opener — Flagged File / Not a Cold Call

"Hey [FIRST NAME], this is Zach — I'm the manager for the mortgage protection department here in [COUNTY/STATE]. Your file for the property at [STREET ADDRESS] just came across my desk — and I want to be clear, I'm not cold calling you. You filled out a card and mailed it in to us requesting information on mortgage protection. Your file is flagged in my system and it's still marked as incomplete."
"Now I know you closed out with [LENDER NAME] a while back — right after that, we sent out letters about your mortgage protection options... that's the coverage that pays off the home if you get sick or pass away. Do you remember seeing that at all?"
"Look — this is my business. This is what I do for a living. And my system is telling me I can save you money. My job is to make sure our clients are best helped — I can't just let your file sit here marked incomplete. It takes 2 minutes to run through a few health and financial questions, then I'll get you on with the Senior Underwriter for a quick 10-minute call. Just to confirm — we're still at [FULL ADDRESS]?"

Step 1b: The Gate Question (Lock It In)

"Pricing requirements changed recently. A lot of coverage bought in the last two years can be replaced for much cheaper today. If Keith can save you a significant amount of money every month while keeping your family fully protected, will you take 5 minutes to speak to him tomorrow?"

Rule: If they say NO to this question, they are a guaranteed no-show. Mark and move on.

Step 2: Financial Inventory (FI) — Data Capture

  • 📍 Confirm address
  • 👤 Spouse/co-borrower on loan?
  • 💰 Current mortgage balance?
  • 📅 Years left on loan?
  • ❤️ Major health conditions?
  • 🚬 Tobacco use?
  • 🎂 Date of birth?

Keep it conversational. You're filling a file, not interrogating.

Step 3: Book The Appointment

"Alright [NAME] — we're going to get you scheduled with Keith Garfinkel, our Senior Underwriter. Would [TIME 1] or [TIME 2] work better for you?"
Give exactly TWO options. Never open-ended.
"Is your [husband/wife] going to be part of this decision? Perfect. Keith requires both of you on the line. What time today or tomorrow are you both home together?"

Step 3b: The Ghost Analogy (Deepen The Why)

"Let's say you passed away yesterday, and I'm talking to your ghost right now. We are looking at this from the outside in. What is your [wife/husband] doing for money to pay the mortgage today?"

Use when they're soft on urgency. Hit the real fear.

Step 4: Security Code (DO NOT SKIP)

"Alright — please grab something to write with real quick. I need to give you your security code for the call."
PAUSE. Wait until they confirm pen in hand. If they won't write — they won't show.
"Your security code is [LAST 4 OF KEITH'S #]. Write down Keith Garfinkel — G-A-R-F-I-N-K-E-L — and [DAY] at [TIME]. Read that back to me."
"We'll be calling you on this number ending in [LAST 4], correct? Do you get texts on this line? Perfect. Set an alarm right now — Keith is blocking that slot specifically for your family."

Step 5: Courtesy Close (Lock Show)

"Keith is currently helping hundreds of families. If an absolute emergency comes up, please be courteous and text this number immediately so we can give your slot to another family who is waiting. Fair enough?"
"Perfect. Keith will talk to you [DAY] at [TIME]. Have a great day!"
Calendar tag: Add "---" before title. SMS only from verified #880. A2P Compliance = non-negotiable.

SECTION 3 — OBJECTION KILL SHEET

Objection: "I Already Have It Taken Care Of"

TRIGGER
"Perfect — which coverage did you go with?" [Ask immediately, shut up. Don't explain.]
WHOLE LIFE?
"Is it because you have health issues? Because whole life is the most expensive product — our Senior Underwriter may be able to get you the same coverage for less with the 2026 rates."
UNIVERSAL/IUL?
"Which company?" → [They answer] → "I know for a fact our Senior Underwriter can save you money. Your file is flagged and still marked incomplete — the guy who wrote your policy left this file incomplete. Every carrier is independent. If they don't mark it in our system, we do not know what you have. That is why we're calling."
"NOT YOUR BUSINESS"
"This IS my business. This is what I do for a living. You mailed in a request for help and I need to make sure you've been helped. My system is flagging your file. Which carrier?"
CIRCLE BACK
"The reason we're calling is 'cause you filled out a card, mailed it into us, requesting help. We need to make sure that you have been helped, right? We need to make sure we're doing our job here."

Objection: "I'm Not Interested"

ATTACK
"Perfect — that's why I'm calling. I'm not wasting your time either, I just need to go over this... [continue with gate question]"

Objection: "I'm Busy / Call Me Later"

IMMEDIATE
"Exactly why I'm calling — I'm not trying to do this whole thing right now. I just need to book you 10 minutes with Keith. What time today or tomorrow?"

Objection: "You Guys Keep Calling Me"

SHARK MODE
"Look, calm down. Those other guys are vultures who bought your data. I have the physical card YOU mailed in right here on my desk. Give me 5 minutes and I'll make sure we never call you again."

Objection: "My Lender Said I Don't Need This"

REFRAME
"Yup — and they're right. THEY don't need you to have it. It only helps YOU, not them."

Objection: "I Never Filled Anything Out"

LIGHTEN IT
"No worries — I don't remember what I had for breakfast either! This is just the coverage that pays off your home if you get sick. Would today or tomorrow work?"

Objection: "Bad Health / Can't Qualify"

AGGRESSIVE
"Really? Let me ask — is your heart beating? [lightheartedly] No worries, we can for sure get you covered."

Objection: "Suspected Spam" Concern

BUILD TRUST
"I can send you Keith's state license and ID right now to verify he's the firm owner. Everything is 100% legit."

SECTION 4 — LIVE TRANSFER PROTOCOL (Warm Transfers)

The Hold Music Warning (CRITICAL)

Leads are hanging up due to unexpected hold music during transfer. Set the stage FIRST, or drops continue.
"I'm going to transfer you to Keith right now. Give me about 15 seconds to connect the line. You are going to hear some brief hold music — please bear with me and do not hang up."

Discord Integration

  • While on call with lead: Ping Discord: "Who is ready?"
  • Response: Martin, Austin, or Keith picks up
  • Intro: Zach introduces lead → Keith confirms → Zach clicks "Leave Call" (never "End Call")

SECTION 5 — NO-SHOW RECOVERY (Live Call)

"Hi [NAME], this is Zachary Dawson. We had a scheduled appointment today with Keith Garfinkel for your mortgage protection review and it looks like we missed each other — no worries."
"Keith still has your file open with the options pulled. Are you available right now for the 10 minutes we need — or would later today or tomorrow morning work better?"
Once rebooked: Run security code AGAIN. Force them to set alarm on spot. No exceptions.
