SECTION 2 — Front-End Scripting & Rebuttals

Zach must not let leads off the phone easily. If they are wishy-washy, attack the gap.

The "I Already Have It / Not Interested" Pivot

"Hold on, John. I'm asking what carrier you have because pricing requirements changed recently. A lot of coverage bought in the last two years can be replaced for much cheaper today. If Keith can save you a significant amount of money every month while keeping your family fully protected, will you take 5 minutes to speak to him tomorrow?"
